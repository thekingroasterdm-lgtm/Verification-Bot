How to Verify

Follow these secure steps to unlock your access.

1

Click Verify

Find the official gateway message in Discord. Click the Verify Identity Now button.

2

Authorize Access

Discord will ask you to confirm. We only request basic identity info to prevent bots. Click Authorize.

3

Return & Enjoy

Once you see the green Success overlay, feel free to close the tab. Your premium channels are already unlocked!

Security Promise

We will never ask for your Discord password or 2FA codes. The entire OAuth process occurs strictly directly on discord.com. Stay safe.
